Smartschool dark design is a browser add-on that makes the Smartschool pages become darkThe extension generates an icon at the top of the screen to turn the dark design on or off.Do you find a bug, or something that is not clearly visible? Go to the link below and create a new "issue".https://github.com/LanderVanlaer/chrome-extension-Smartschool/issues*Can only be used by schools that have login details for Smartschool*.
